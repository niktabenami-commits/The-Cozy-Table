Our Story

A Taste of Home
in Houston

"More than just a café — we're a warm corner of the Philippines right here in Houston."

Welcome to The Cozy Table by Kâpé Tayo — Houston's first and only Filipino coffee shop. Our café is built on the belief that food and coffee are the languages of love and community.

From our rich, aromatic Kape Filipino to hearty silog plates that taste like home-cooked meals, every cup and every dish is made with heart — honoring the flavors of the Philippines while welcoming everyone to our table.
